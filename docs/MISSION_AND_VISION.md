### Mission

My mission is to restore the "Survival" in Minecraft. I aim to transform the game from an infinite resource loop into a meaningful experience where every item, crop, and action has value. ### Vision

I strive to be a pioneer of high-complexity, open-source addons for Minecraft Bedrock. My goal is to break the "inferiority complex" of the platform by proving that technical restrictions are just catalysts for creative innovation.

### Core Pillars

- **Vanilla Integrity:** I don't add bloat; I enhance existing systems to feel like official updates.
- **Performance for All:** Optimization is an act of empathy. High-end simulation for low-end devices.
- **Radical Openness:** I believe that hiding code hinders progress. I share my systems (GPL-3.0) to elevate the entire Bedrock community.

### Design Principles

1.  **Meaningful Complexity:** If a mechanic is added, it must deepen the game loop, not just add clicks.
2.  **Sustainable Exploration:** Systems like "Offline Growth" are designed to give the player the freedom to roam without being a slave to their base.
3.  **Systemic Cohesion:** Every addon in this series must interact with the others to create a unified, challenging world.

### About the Author

> **A Labor of Love**
